One door closes, another opens

2020 was a rough year for many, myself included. I was fortunate to keep my job for a few months after lockdowns began in March. My team and I worked from home until the organization decided to lay us off. As difficult as it was to leave a job I had been with for 5 years, I knew I wanted something different, something more than what my old position offered me.

After some time off, I jumped into job searching. I happened to see an ad for a coding boot camp while scrolling through social media and my interest was piqued. I did some research, and before too long I had signed up for a workshop series with SheCodes. Taking those first steps into web development taught me that I love to code!

I am looking for any junior web developer positions or freelance work that will allow me to to flex my skills and continue to increase my coding knowledge while gaining valuable experience.
