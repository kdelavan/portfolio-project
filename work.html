Weather App

A fully functional weather app that pulls data for over 200,000 cities across the globe. Search for your city, or simply click the 'Use location' button to see the current temperature, weather description, humidity, wind speed and an hourly forecast. You can even toggle between celsius and fahrenheit, depending on which unit you prefer. Coded with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
